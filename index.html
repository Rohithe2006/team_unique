APPLICATION for prototype1(general purpose)

1.Indoor air quality
CO2 sensors can help ensure proper ventilation and maintain
optimal indoor air quality in offices, schools, hospitals,
and other buildings. 

2.HVAC systems
CO2 sensors can optimize energy efficiency by adjusting 
airflow based on occupancy and CO2 concentration.

3.Greenhouses and agriculture
CO2 sensors can enhance plant photosynthesis and growth.

4.Medical practice
CO2 sensors can be used to monitor blood gases in patients,
 providing clues about their respiratory and circulatory state.

5.Building management
CO2 sensors can be used to automate building controls based on 
real-time usage of spaces.

APPLICATION for prototype 2(indoore)

1.Indoor air quality (IAQ) monitoring
CO2 is a key component of IAQ monitoring, which can help improve 
breathing.
                
2.Ventilation management
CO2 sensors can estimate the amount of fresh air required based 
on the conditions in individual rooms. They can send a signal 
to a ventilation device or variable air volume system (VAV) 
to adjust ventilation levels. 
                
3.Human occupancy counting
Measuring CO2 levels indirectly determines how many people 
are in a room, and ventilation can be adjusted accordingly.
